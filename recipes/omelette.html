Omelette

Make an Omelette

Making an Omelette is a convenient way to make breakfast since it only takes just 10 minutes of your time! Omelette is not just an easy way to make breakfast but also helps you start your day with a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the eggs into a bowl and whisk them well
  2. Sprinkle some salt over the whisked egg mixture
  3. Pour the mixture into a non-stick pan under very low heat and add butter to the pan
  4. Wait until the edges of the egg sets and then move the eggs to the oven
  5. Bake the egg for 4 minutes under very low heat
  6. Add butter to the omelette to allow it to cool

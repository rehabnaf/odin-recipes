How to make Scrambled Eggs

Making scrambled eggs is a convenient way to make breakfast since it only takes just 10 minutes of your time! Scrambled eggs is not just an easy way to make breakfast but also helps you start your day with a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the eggs into a bowl and whisk them well
  2. Sprinkle some salt over the whisked egg mixture
  3. Pour the mixture into a non-stick pan under very low heat and add butter to the pan
  4. Wait until the egg sets (usually around 2 minutes) and then start scrambling the eggs
  5. After two minutes, remove the scrambled eggs from the pan
